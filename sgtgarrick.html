Sergeant Kyle "Gaz" Garrick

Kyle "Gaz" Garrick is a skilled and reliable SAS operative featured in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II and Modern Warfare III. Known for his calm demeanor and tactical expertise, Gaz plays a crucial role in supporting Task Force 141’s high-risk missions. His steady leadership and sharp combat skills make him a trusted teammate, contributing significantly to the team’s efforts to neutralize global threats and maintain international security.
