Captain John Price

Captain John Price is a seasoned, no-nonsense SAS operator and leader of Task Force 141 in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II and Modern Warfare III. Renowned for his tactical brilliance, iconic boonie hat, and gravelly voice, Price is fiercely loyal to his team and unrelenting in hunting global threats like the ultranationalists and terrorists threatening world stability. In these games, he navigates morally gray decisions and high-stakes missions, embodying the hardened, principled soldier willing to do whatever it takes to stop catastrophic attacks.
