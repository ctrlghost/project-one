Lieutenant Simon "Ghost" Riley

Simon “Ghost” Riley is an elite, enigmatic operative in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II and Modern Warfare III, known for his iconic skull-patterned balaclava and dry, sardonic wit. As a key member of Task Force 141, Ghost is highly skilled, fiercely loyal, and brutally efficient, specializing in covert operations and direct assaults alike. Beneath his masked, intimidating exterior lies a soldier shaped by betrayal and loss, driven by duty and an unyielding resolve to eliminate threats to global security.
