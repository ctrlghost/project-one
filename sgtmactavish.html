Sergeant Johnathan "Soap" MacTavish

John “Soap” MacTavish is a tough, sharp-witted sergeant in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II and Modern Warfare III, serving as a trusted member of Task Force 141. Known for his signature mohawk and fearless attitude, Soap excels in demolition, close-quarters combat, and tactical planning. He forms a strong bond with Captain Price and Ghost, proving himself as a dependable, courageous soldier who’s willing to take on the most dangerous missions to stop global threats.
